About


SP Balasubrahmanyam, the man behind some of the most beautiful voices in the world.



→  Won six National Film Awards


→  Worked predominantly in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Hindi, and Malayalam films


→  held the Guinness World Record for recording the highest number of songs by a singer with over 40,000 songs


→  recipient of the Padma Shri (2001) and Padma Bhushan (2011) from the Government of India.


→  recorded 21 songs in Kannada for the composer Upendra Kumar in Bengaluru from 9 am to 9 pm in a day


→  honoured with the Silver Peacock Medal as Indian Film Personality of the Year


→  Passed away in Chennai from post COVID-19 complications
